BIS / CRS is mandatory for Electronics Games (Video)

IS Standard : IS 616

Electronics Video games are very popular form of entertainment all around the world. It is necessary to acquire a BIS-CRS registration for every electronics video game that is brought into India. The procedure is simple and easy, ensuring that the gaming system fulfills all safety and quality standards set by the Bureau of Indian Standards.

The BIS / CRS Registration Scheme is required for all Electronics Games (Video) under the IS standard - IS 616. Electronics Games (Video) may not be imported or sold in the Indian consumer market without the BIS registration.

Brand Liaison provides comprehensive BIS Registration consultancy services for Electronics Video Games from beginning to end, including assisting you with all documentation formalities and liaising with the BIS Office in India.
